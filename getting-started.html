Let's Get Ready

Here is a guide to getting started.

Step 1: Assemble Basic Tools

Find the tools and materials

To get started with any sewing project you will need these essentials:

  • Thread - one or more spools - pick the colours you like!
  • Needle
  • Fabric - check out the materials list for your project
  • A box to keep your tools and materials - a shoebox will do!

Step 2: Find a Space

A good space to work will have:

  • Good light (it could be near a window, a well-lit room, or near a desk-light)
  • A surface to lay out your materials
  • A place that doesn't block a walkway or the stairs
